🛒 Superstore Sales — Exploratory Data Analysis
Overview
An end-to-end EDA project analyzing sales, profit, and customer behavior across a fictional US superstore. The goal was to answer one key question: where is the business making and losing money?
Key Findings

Technology is the top category in both sales and profit
Furniture generates high sales but nearly zero profit — a pricing problem
Tables, Bookcases, and Supplies are operating at a loss
Discounts above 20% consistently push profit into negative territory
Q4 outperforms all other quarters every year — strong seasonality
West region leads in both sales and profit; Central underperforms

Tools & Skills

Python (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, numpy)
Data cleaning & feature engineering
Univariate & multivariate analysis
Time-series trend analysis
Business insight storytelling
